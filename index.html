This is a game that tests your typing speed while teaching you the definitions of words and phrases used in the QTIPoC community.
To begin, scroll down until you see a single word/phrase followed and start typing the definition below.
Remember to use uppercase(capital) letters whenever they appear.
NOTE: This does not work on Safari or touch screen devices. All forward slashes(/), quotation marks(") and apostrophes(')have been replaced by an exclaimation mark (!).
