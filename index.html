This is a game that tests your typing speed while teaching you the definitions of words and phrases used to define people, circumstances and occurences members of the QTIPoC community face, most in their day to day lives.
To BEGIN:, scroll down until you see a single word/phrase followed by a sentence or paragraph and type what you see!
Remember to use uppercase(capital) letters whenever they appear.
NOTE: This does not work on Safari or touch screen devices. All forward slashes(/), quotation marks(") and apostrophes(')have been replaced by an exclaimation mark (!).
